About

Feel art in science, know science in art

I'm Viktória Fröhlich, an astronomer and research assistant at the HUN-REN Konkoly Observatory. My work sits in dynamical and computational astrophysics — the dynamical consequences of stellar mass loss in multi-star and planetary systems, the disks around polluted white dwarfs, and rings and moons of minor planets.

Scientific vision

In my professional work I'm someone who wants to connect robust theory results to clear observables in papers that tell a story.

Artistic vision

I want to bring people closer to the Cosmos and show them that science and art are really just the two sides of the same coin.

Two ways to wonder about the beauties of the same sky — one that analyses, one that simply looks — both with vision, presence and an open horizon.

I play the guitar, ride a bike, and love to read. Quite the Star Wars fan.
